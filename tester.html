Mild allergic reactions can cause symptoms like skin itching, redness, hives, or mild swelling. These are often triggered by food, insect bites, pollen, or medications. Stay calm and identify the allergen if possible. Avoid further contact and provide comfort until symptoms improve.

  • Latex or chemical contact

  • Apply a cool compress to soothe itching or redness
  • Encourage rest and hydration

Preventing allergic reactions involves identifying triggers, avoiding exposure, and taking care of your skin and environment.

üß¥ Maintain Good Skin Care
