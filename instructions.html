Emergency Ready: Life-Saving Skills with Nothing but You!

Quick Fixes, No Gear Needed: Life-Saving Actions with Just Your Hands and Head

In everyday emergencies, the most powerful tools are already with you—your hands and your head. From stopping bleeding to clearing airways or calming someone in distress, quick, confident action can make all the difference. No gadgets, no panic—just smart, simple steps that save lives. Be ready, anytime, anywhere.

👶 Infant Choking (Under 1 Year Old)

👶 5 back blows ➕ 5 chest thrusts = Infant safe! ❌ No Heimlich!
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Choking Rescue for Teens & Adults

👋➕🤜 Give back blows and abdominal thrusts (Heimlich) until the airway is clear

🤕 Nosebleeds

Tilt head forward, pinch nostrils, apply cold compress if available.
😵 Fainting / Dizziness

Lay flat, raise legs, loosen clothing, ensure fresh air.
🩹 Minor Cuts & Scrapes

Clean with water, press to stop bleeding, cover with cloth/bandage.
🔥 Minor Burns

Run cool water 10–15 mins, cover loosely, no ice/butter.
⚡ Seizure (Epilepsy)

Lay on side, remove harmful objects, do not hold down, time seizure.
🤧 Allergic Reaction (Mild)

Calm person, remove allergen, watch for worsening signs.
🌬️ Asthma Attack

Keep calm, sit upright, encourage slow breathing, avoid triggers.
🩹 Splinters & Stuck Bits

Wash the area, gently remove the splinter with clean tweezers, clean the spot again, and cover it with a bandage.
☀️ Heat Exhaustion / Sunstroke

Move to cooler area, give sips of water, apply wet cloth, loosen clothes.
😰 Hyperventilation / Panic Attack

Talk calmly, guide breathing slowly, count together, reassure.
💧 Dehydration

Give small sips of water or ORS, rest in cool environment.
🐝 Insect Bites / Bee Stings

Remove stinger, wash, apply cold compress, observe for reactions.
